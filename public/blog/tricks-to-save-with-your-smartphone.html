Tricks to save with your smartphone

Saving can be much easier if you follow a series of simple tricks. It all starts with that device in your hand. There is much more you can do than you think.

Start by uninstalling those shopping apps that constantly tempt you If you don't want to uninstall them, you can always remove them from your home screen and take them to your library.You can also create a Focus profile where you don't have these apps enabled and set it during your savings periods.

Remove the cards of your virtual wallet. When you want to buy it will be the first barrier you encounter. Delete the numbers of your cards from accounts in online stores. It is not allowed to borrow from your friends.

Delivery applications are another source of temptation. In addition to the fact that junk food is very harmful to your health, you will save a lot of money by having a healthy dinner with products that you have at home. Uninstall these applications of your smartphone as soon as possible.

Do you need all the subscriptions you have? Some of them are duplicates or are from services that you have not used for a long time. Review the list of your subscriptions and delete those you no longer need. Find time for yourself and skip one of those series.

In these times, we spend a lot of time at home or in the office. Surely, you are all the time connected to Wi-Fi. Check the data plan of your operator and reduce it to a cheaper one. You don't need that many gigabytes. Take advantage and recover all that time you spend on social networks.

With this series of tips and the determined use of Belt, you will be able to save very easily and achieve your goals sooner than you think. Cheer up.
